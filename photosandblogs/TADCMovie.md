## TADC Movie

*June 7 2026 7:42am*
I  watched The Amazing Digital Circus Movie yesterday. It was so good :D

I love how the movie ties everything together while still leaving room for theorizing (like about the real life folk and stuff). It really is a solid end to finish the series on. Like, the main antagonist(caine) learns his lesson and grows as a character, the main characters get as happy as an ending as they could have gotten, and we find out basically everything we need to know about the circus.

I'm a bit conflicted about it's showing in the theater. On one hand, it's definitely the highest quality version of the movie I could have watched, but on the other, I had to deal with the other fans reactions.

I'll say that the circus fandom is basically the same online as in real life (except they don't hate gooseworks as much). So like it's a bunch of shippers, there was a furry and a cosplayer (even though it wasn't opening night), and overall they seemed very excited for this finale of a series that they were probably following along for from the start. Which i understand and relate to, but it means that every time something mildly exciting happens, you just hear lots of noise from the audience when you're just trying to watch a movie in peace. At least it wasn't like the minecraft movie, and the seat next to me was empty, so no awkward interactions for *this* semi-introvert!

My favourite characters are definitely the fish and bubble. Out of the main cast prolly kinger or caine
